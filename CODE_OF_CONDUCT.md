# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and professional.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Provide actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to `alammoosa07@gmail.com`. Maintainers will review and respond promptly.
